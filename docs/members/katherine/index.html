Bio

A cheese-stick and dog loving Philly girl, Katherine is a first year student who could not feel more at home with her new family, the Roaring 20. She is an alto who loves the classic movies such as Zoolander, Dodgeball and Dumb and Dumber, and her favorite hang-out spot is the local Costco. When she isn’t singing she loves hiking, reading, playing squash and tennis, and cheering on the Phillies. She enjoys movie scores and fart jokes, and is currently a pre-med student, if the Princeton science courses don’t scare her out of it. Katherine is known back home as the girl who sings more than she talks and the one who sneezes three times in a row without fail. She sits watching every Community episode on OnDemand for hours and The Far Side, goldfish, and Pearl Jam never fail to make her smile.
