Booking

Each year The Roaring 20 gives a number of concerts and shorter performances at a wide variety of venues. No matter what the event, the fine sound and spirited performance of The Roaring 20 will make for an unforgettable experience. Its members come dressed to impress, and each performance is specifically tailored to suit the audience’s particular tastes. Due to The Roaring 20’s broad repertoire and universal appeal, audiences of all ages take great delight in a Roaring 20 performance.

The Roaring 20 is available for performances within driving distance of Princeton during the academic year, and travels nationally and internationally several times a year when school is not in session. If you would like further information regarding booking the Roaring 20 for your event, contact us!
